# School_District_Analysis
## Overview of School District Analysis
Maria is the chief data scientist for a city school district. She has requested an analysis of school spending and standardized test scores.
## Results
## District Summary
The average math score and average reading score stayed the same. The percentage of students passing math increased by 0.8% as did the percentage of students passing reading. The overall passing percentage also increased by 1%.

## Scores by School Spending 
The $630-644 bin saw a increase in the passing percentages since Thomas High School was in that spending range. 
## Scores by School Size
The Medium (1000-2000) bin saw a increase in the passing percentages since Thomas High School was in that school size.
## Scores by School Type
The Charter schools saw a increase in the passing percentages since Thomas High School was in that school type. 

## Summary 
- Schools that spend between 585-615 dollars per student have a better percent passing reading, percent passing math, and percent overall passing rate than schools that spend more than 615 dollars per student. 
- The smaller the school, the less they spend per student.
- Charter schools have a higher percent passing reading, percent passing math, and percent overall passing rate than District schools.
- In all 15 schools, the Average Reading Score is higher than the Average Math Score.
- For the top 5 performing schools, the percent passing math is about 20 percent lower than the percent passing reading.
